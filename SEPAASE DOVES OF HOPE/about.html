About Doves Of Hope And Squeakers Of Hope Pathfinder Club

Guiding young people to grow in spirit, mind, and body

Our Mission

The Pathfinder Club is a worldwide program of the Seventh-day Adventist Church designed to empower young people to develop leadership, teamwork, and service skills. Through camping, community service, and spiritual growth, we help youth become responsible citizens and devoted members of society.

We strive to nurture character, encourage discipline, and inspire excellence in every member.

Discipline

We teach members to live structured, responsible, and principled lives.

Leadership

Empowering young people to lead with integrity and vision.

Service

Encouraging community service and helping others selflessly.
